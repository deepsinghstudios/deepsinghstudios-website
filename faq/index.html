FAQ

Straight answers about booking, team headshot days, event coverage and travel.

How Far In Advance Should I Book?

Two to three weeks is ideal for headshot sessions, especially for teams of 10 or more. Event photography can often be booked with less notice depending on availability. The sooner you reach out, the more flexibility we have with scheduling.

Do You Come To Our Office?

Yes. I bring a mobile studio setup to your location for team headshot days. All I need is a room with enough space and good natural light or overhead lighting. I handle the rest: backdrops, lighting, camera equipment.

How Long Does A Team Headshot Day Take?

Plan on about 5 to 10 minutes per person for individual shots. A team of 14 people usually takes about two hours including setup and transitions between departments or groups.

What If Someone On The Team Is Out That Day?

No problem. I can schedule a separate mini-session for anyone who missed the main day. We'll coordinate a time that works.

When Will We Receive The Photos?

Delivery timing depends on the size of the job and how your team plans to use the images. We will talk through that before booking. If you need a few images quickly for social media, press or an internal announcement, let me know upfront so I can plan for it.

Do You Offer Event Photography?

Yes. Conferences, galas, trade shows, award nights, and corporate events are covered at $325 per hour with a two-hour minimum. I also offer on-site headshot stations for conferences and corporate events.

Do You Travel Outside Metro Detroit?

Yes. I cover Metro Detroit, Toledo, Grand Rapids, and Fort Wayne with no travel fees. For locations beyond that, travel is billed separately.

What's Your Cancellation Policy?

I require 48 hours notice for cancellations. This allows me to keep the day open for other teams who may need it.
