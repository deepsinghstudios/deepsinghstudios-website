About

The Photographer Behind The Work

I started Deep Singh Studios in 2015 because I believed corporate photography could be better than a checklist.

Before that, I spent years watching photo days unfold the same way: the photographer shows up late, sets up in the way, rushes people through, then disappears for weeks. The result? Headshots that look like driver's license photos and event galleries where half the important moments were missed.

I built a different kind of practice. One where preparation matters more than gear. Where showing up early is non-negotiable. Where I learn your schedule before I walk through the door so I never slow you down.

Over the years, that approach has taken me into boardrooms, convention centers, law firms, hospitals, and nonprofit galas across Michigan and beyond. The settings change. The standard doesn't.

You hire a photographer so you don't have to manage one. I work quietly, move deliberately, and deliver images that are ready when you need them.

When I'm not shooting, I'm usually editing, studying light, or spending time with my family in Metro Detroit. The same discipline I bring to a shoot is what keeps me grounded day to day.

Thanks for being here. If you think we might be a fit, I'd love to hear what you're working on.
