About Deep Singh Studios

I am Deep Singh, a corporate headshot and event photographer based in Metro Detroit.

My work is built around making the photography part of the day easy for the client. A team headshot day or an event already has plenty of moving parts. The photography should be organized, clear and easy to work around.

My experience includes nonprofit galas, real estate agency teams and corporate dinners. The work changes from job to job, but the preparation stays the same.

Before a shoot, I want to know who is involved, what needs to be covered, how the images will be used and which moments cannot be missed. On the day, I arrive early, check the equipment, set up the lighting and stay in communication with the person running the schedule.

For headshots, I keep the process relaxed and efficient. I guide people through it, make a quick conversation and give nervous people a moment to settle in. The goal is a strong, consistent image without making anyone feel like they are standing in front of the camera forever.

For events, I work quietly and stay out of the way while watching for the moments that matter: speakers, guest reactions, real interaction, the setup and the details the team worked hard to create.

The focus is straightforward: show up prepared, make the experience easy and create professional images your team can actually use.

If you are planning team headshots, a headshot station or event coverage, tell me what you have coming up and I will help you figure out the right setup.
