How To Prepare For Your Corporate Headshot Day

A good headshot day is simple when everyone knows what to expect. You do not need to overthink it, but a little preparation makes the final images cleaner and more consistent.

Choose Simple Clothing

Wear solid colors and avoid busy patterns. Small stripes, tight checks, shiny fabrics, and large logos can pull attention away from your face. If your company has brand colors, work within that palette, but keep the outfit clean.

Bring a couple outfit options if you are unsure. A jacket, second shirt, or different top can give you flexibility without slowing the session down.

Handle The Details The Night Before

Iron or steam your clothes the night before. Wrinkles show up quickly on camera, especially under studio lighting.

Get a good night’s sleep. It sounds obvious, but it shows in your eyes, posture, and energy. Arrive with a clean, moisturized face and keep makeup natural unless your normal professional look is more polished.

Know The Timing

For team headshot days, plan on about 5 to 10 minutes per person. That includes getting settled, making small adjustments, shooting a few options, and moving to the next person.

The session will not feel stiff. I will guide you through posture, expression, and where to look, so you are not left guessing what to do with your face or hands.

Keep It Natural

The best headshots still look like you. The goal is not to create a different version of yourself. It is to make sure you look prepared, approachable, and professional in the places where people first meet you online.
