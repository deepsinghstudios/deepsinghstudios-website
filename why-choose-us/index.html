Why Choose Us

Corporate headshots and event photography come down to preparation, clear communication and being easy to work with. I plan ahead, show up ready and keep the process moving.

I bring the studio to you. Backdrops, lighting, camera gear - it all fits in the car and sets up in any room with decent space. Your team does not need to travel. You do not need to find a photographer. I show up, set up, and get it done in a few hours.

Delivery needs are part of the conversation before we book. If you need a few images quickly for social media, press or an internal announcement, tell me upfront so I can plan for it.

No travel fees across Metro Detroit, Toledo, Grand Rapids, or Fort Wayne. Volume pricing starts at $100 per person with a 10-person minimum. Event coverage starts at $325 per hour with a 2-hour minimum. Rates are transparent and listed on the pricing page, no surprises and no back and forth.

Consistency matters. Every headshot in a team gallery should look like it was taken in the same session. Same lighting, same crop and the same retouching standard help the team look coordinated wherever those images are used.
