Why Choose Us

Corporate headshots and event photography are not complicated. Show up prepared. Be easy to work with. Deliver on time. Most photographers stumble on at least one of those. I don't.

I bring the studio to you. Backdrops, lighting, camera gear - it all fits in the car and sets up in any room with decent space. Your team does not need to travel. You do not need to find a photographer. I show up, set up, and get it done in a few hours.

Turnaround is straightforward. Individual headshots land in your gallery within 24 hours. Team days deliver inside 72 hours. Event galleries go out in 48 to 72 hours. If you need it faster, I can make that happen.

No travel fees across Metro Detroit, Toledo, Grand Rapids, or Fort Wayne. Volume pricing starts at $100 per person with a 10-person minimum. Event coverage starts at $325 per hour with a 2-hour minimum. Rates are transparent and listed on the pricing page, no surprises and no back and forth.

Consistency matters. Every headshot in a team gallery should look like it was taken in the same session by the same photographer. Because it was. Same lighting, same crop, same retouch. That is the difference between a professional team page and one that looks thrown together.
