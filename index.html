About the Studio

About Deep Singh Studios

You hire a photographer so you don't have to babysit one. I show up prepared, work without drama, and deliver images that serve their purpose — professional, usable, and ready when you need them.

Disciplined Professional Dependable

I started Deep Singh Studios in 2015 after realizing most corporate photography felt transactional — show up, shoot, deliver, done. I wanted to build something different: a practice where discipline and precision produce images that actually serve a purpose.

That means arriving early. It means understanding your event schedule before I step through the door. It means capturing the keynote that just got moved to a different room, the candid networking moment that wasn't on the shot list, and the executive portrait your CEO needs before she catches her flight. I do the job required — and the ten things you didn't think to ask for.

Available Nationwide

Based in Metro Detroit and available to travel nationwide. Whether your conference is in Houston, your headquarters is in New York, your summit is in Los Angeles, or your team event is in Chicago — I bring the full studio setup to you. Dallas. San Francisco. Atlanta. Denver. Boston. Wherever your business takes you.

From a single executive portrait to a 500-person corporate event, the approach is the same: arrive prepared, work efficiently, deliver images that make your team and your organization look like the professionals they are.
