The Problem

Events Deserve Great Photos.

Your team can spend months planning a conference, gala, or headshot day, only for the final images to feel like an afterthought. Deep Singh Studios solves that with prepared, reliable corporate photography for headshots, events, and on-site headshot stations.

Our Services

Corporate Photography Built For Real Business Needs.

Corporate Headshots

Clean, consistent portraits for law firms, insurance agencies, real estate offices, nonprofits, and growing teams. Sessions are built around your brand with consistent lighting, natural retouching, and fast delivery.

Event Photography

Conference, gala, trade show, and award-night coverage across Metro Detroit and beyond. From keynote speeches to candid networking moments, every frame is captured with intention and delivered quickly.

Headshot Stations

On-site headshot stations for conferences and events. Attendees walk in, get photographed, and receive polished headshots instantly or within 24 hours, with consistent cropping and retouching for every person.
